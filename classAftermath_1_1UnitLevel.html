Detailed Description

A UnitLevel is the level of a TileGroupUnit.

It has a cost that is required for automatic upgrade at a ProductionCenter.

Constructs a new UnitLevel with the given name, description, and image.

The level can be upgraded automatically at a ProductionCenter for its auto upgrade cost.

Parameters:
name- The name of the new level.
description- A brief description of the new level.
image- The image of the unit level.
upgradeCost- The cost to upgrade to this level.
autoUpgradeCost- The cost to automatically upgrade to this level at a ProductionCenter (as the unit is built).
power- The power of this unit. This is used in combat.
cargo- The amount of cargo that this unit can carry. This is added to the merchant marine of its owner.
